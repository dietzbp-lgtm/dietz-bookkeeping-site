How to Grant Accountant Access in QuickBooks Online

Follow these steps to invite an accounting firm to your QuickBooks Online file.

Schedule a Consultation Back to Resources

Step-by-step

  1. Log into QuickBooks Online.
  2. Click Settings (Gear icon).
  3. Select Manage Users.
  4. Go to the Accounting Firms tab.
  5. Enter the firm ID (or invite details provided by your accountant).
  6. Click Invite.
